Our Wedding


Eindani and Benjamin Carter

March 25th, 2021
Central Park, New York
Maid of Honor: Ella Hunter
Best Men: Joshua and Jacob Carter


A small wedding in the Big Apple.

       Endani and Ben place family among their highest personal values, and many of our friends have been like an extended family to us over the years. We love all of you very much.

       Unfortunately, the pandemic has rendered planning large gatherings uncertain. What's more, Eindani and Ben are both graduate students with limited budgets. For these reasons, hosting a large traditional wedding with all of our family and friends is not safe or financially feasible at this time. Instead, we planned an intimate wedding with immediate family to be held at Cop Cot in Central Park.

       It is our goal to host a large anniversary celebration with all of our loved ones in Naples, Florida. In the meantime, we hope to bring everyone a little closer to our special day through this webpage. After the wedding, we promise to upload photos and videos of the day for all to enjoy. We hope this can help connect each of us until we can all be united in the same location.

Please return to this webpage in April for documentation of our special day!
